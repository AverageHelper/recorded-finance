Accountable To Yourself.

Keep a close eye on your spending. Never lose a dime again! If you're the sort to obsess about where your money is going, this might be the tool for you.

Your Ledger, Not Mine.

Recorded Finance, by design, cannot see your data, even on our own servers. We don't need or want to see what you do with your money. That's not my job. (I'd rather you not do illegal stuff with this.)

Keep Me Accountable.

All of our source code is open to read. Feel free to open an issue or submit a pull request on this project's GitHub page. Let me know if anything needs fixed.
